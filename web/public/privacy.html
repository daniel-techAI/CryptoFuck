Privacy policy

Effective July 20, 2026. NOCTURNE is an open-source crypto research and paper-trading application.

Guest use

Without signing in, paper trades and preferences stay in your browser's local storage. The public market snapshot is downloaded from the hosted app.

Cloud profiles

If cloud profiles are enabled and you sign in, Supabase Auth processes your email address and authentication session. NOCTURNE stores your user ID, display name, optional handle, avatar URL, paper-account data, simulated orders, and preferences. Your email is not copied into the public profile table.

Google data

Google sign-in requests only identity scopes: OpenID, email, and basic profile. NOCTURNE does not request, read, or store Gmail messages, contacts, Drive files, or Google passwords.

Sharing and security

Profile data is stored by the configured Supabase project. Row-level security limits private account, order, and preference data to the signed-in owner. Basic profiles are readable only to authenticated users. No personal data is sold.

Retention and deletion

Cloud data remains until the account owner chooses Delete cloud profile in the profile dialog. Deletion removes the auth identity and cascades to its profile, paper orders, account state, and preferences. Guest data can be removed through the browser's site-data controls.

Risk and changes

NOCTURNE is research software, not financial advice. This policy may change with the app; the effective date above identifies the current version. The source and change history are available on GitHub.
